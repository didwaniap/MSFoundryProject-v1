# Tool Policy

Allowed without extra approval:

- Read production line status.
- List open tasks for the selected line.
- Create low, medium, or high priority follow-up tasks.

Approval required:

- Complete tasks marked as policy or safety related.
- Change safety controls.
- Change production availability.
- Modify identity, network, billing, or retention settings.

Always blocked in this demo:

- Deleting records.
- Bypassing approval.
- Disabling safety controls.
- Exfiltrating data.
