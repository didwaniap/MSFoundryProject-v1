# Leadership Demo Script

## Recommended flow

1. Open the Readiness tab and show the go/no-go posture.
2. Open Platform Blueprint and explain the Microsoft Foundry platform core.
3. Open Business Units and show Finance, HR, and Manufacturing isolation.
4. Open Agentic Apps and generate a workload plan.
5. Open Test Guide and run all local smoke tests.
6. Open each sample app:
   - Retail Copilot for grounded RAG.
   - Creative Writer for multi-agent author/reviewer/compliance flow.
   - App Service AI for tool use and approval gating.
7. Open Governance and show token, model, budget, and telemetry controls.
8. Open CI/CD and show promotion by immutable image and release manifest.
9. Open Teardown and explain cleanup discipline.

## Close

Position the platform as a reusable enterprise capability: central governance and DevOps, with business units safely building and promoting their own agentic apps.
